About Us

Mission

We would like to give back to our community by teaching kids swimming skills, as it is an important life skill to have. We also want to help kids improve their swimming abilities - whether it is to just be able to play in the pool or swim faster.

Who We Are

We are a group of competitive swimmers from the top clubs in the nation. We are dedicated to providing valuable swim lessons and water safety education in our local communities. Swim with a splash has been an ongoing event since 2023, and we are passionate to help others learn and grow.

Why Choose Us

  • We prioritize the child's safety at all times, with at least 2 coaches per child.
  • Our private lessons are customized to every child's needs, and we assign coaches based on the information provided to us.
  • We understand children's needs and are able to use that to help them succeed.
  • We provide feedback to parents after every class

Programs

Learn‑to‑Swim

This is geared towards children who want to learn how to swim or are in the elementary steps.

Stroke Clinic

If your child is proficient in their strokes, we will help polish your child's skills to bring them to competitive standards.
