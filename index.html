About Us

Mission

We would like to give back to our community by teaching kids swimming skills, as it is an important life skill to have. We also want to help kids improve their swimming abilities - whether it is to just be able to play in the pool or swim faster.

Who We Are

We are a group of competitive swimmers from the top clubs in the nation. We are dedicated to providing valuable swim lessons and water safety education in our local communities. Swim with a splash has been an ongoing event for the past 2 years and counting, and we are passionate to help others learn and grow.

Why Choose Us

  • We provide feedback to parents after every class
  • Our private lessons are customized to every child's needs, and we assign coaches based on the information provided to us.
  • We prioritize the child's safety at all times, with at least 2 coaches per child.
  • We understand children's needs and are able to use that to help them succeed.

Programs

Learn‑to‑Swim (Ages 5–8)

First, we will teach them to float on their backs. In swimming, being able to float is the number 1 priority. Second, we will teach them how to kick. We will provide kick boards, so you don't need to worry about that.

Stroke Clinic (Ages 9–12)

If your child knows how to swim, we will help polish skills that your child have.
